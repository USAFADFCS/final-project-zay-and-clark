Category: exfiltration | Subtype: connection to known exfiltration endpoint

Purpose: Recommended actions when logs indicate connection to known exfiltration endpoint under exfiltration.

TLDR: Follow containment, investigation, eradication, and hardening steps.

High level actions:
- TLDR: Block outbound channels and preserve evidence.
- Confirm: Large transfers, SFTP sessions, or connections to known exfil endpoints.
- Contain: Block destinations at firewall or proxy and isolate the host.
- Investigate: Determine data volume and destination. Check cloud storage and tunneling.
- Eradicate and recover: Remove tools and rotate keys. Follow breach notification procedures.
- Hardening: Egress filtering, DLP, and rate limiting large transfers.

Subtype specific steps:
- Block destinations and capture PCAP if possible.

Useful commands:
$ nethogs -t | head
$ iftop -t -s 1 | head
$ ss -tpn | grep -E 'ssh|sftp|rsync|http|https'

Notes: Adjust commands for your distro. Preserve evidence before altering the system for forensics when severity is high.
